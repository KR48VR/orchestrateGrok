## Query 1: Summarize All-In Podcast’s view on AI coding agents

### Normal Mode (Fast & Conversational)
All-In Podcast is very bullish on AI coding agents. Recent episodes with Marc Benioff highlight how tools from Anthropic (Claude) are making coding dramatically cheaper and faster. Benioff says he's spending ~$300M this year on Anthropic and that agents let Salesforce do things they couldn't before — humans + agents working together. 

They see agents disrupting SaaS (some companies will thrive by focusing on customers/innovation, others will die if they don't adapt). Jensen Huang clip: every engineer will soon have 100 agents, shifting work to creativity and orchestration rather than raw coding. 

**Overall tone**: Massive efficiency gains, but it will reshape software companies and valuations.
